About

Sprout & Grow is a greenhouse nursery in Raleigh, NC, specializing in high-quality organic plants. The nursery aims to provide customers with a diverse selection of meticulously nurtured plants that promote sustainable gardening practices. Sprout & Grow prioritizes transparency, integrity, and environmental stewardship, offering a range of educational resources and personalized support to empower customers in cultivating thriving organic gardens. The nursery is committed to transparent and ethical practices, fostering sustainability and environmental stewardship within the community.
